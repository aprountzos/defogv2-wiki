What is Defog v2?

Defog v2 automatically distributes services across Kubernetes clusters with zero manual intervention. It combines two intelligent strategies:

  • Stress Strategy - Immediate traffic shifting (5-10s)
  • RPS Strategy - Long-term service placement (60-90s)
